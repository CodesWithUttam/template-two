Market analysis is the process of evaluating market conditions to identify trends, opportunities, and potential risks. It involves understanding customer needs, analyzing the competitive landscape, and assessing economic factors. This comprehensive evaluation enables businesses to make informed decisions, develop effective strategies, and position themselves for growth and competitive advantage.
